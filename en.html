About

The intensity of our lifestyle can cause some discomfort. The stress, lack of energy, tension, anxiety, pain, all these can disrupt our health balance Different massage techniques can help to relax, while improving the recovery of our body's energy. Massage promotes better sleep, blood circulation, reduces stress, can relieve tension and pain, and helps to find our Health Balance.

Our Services

Swedish
Massage

Intramuscular massage, that banishes tensions, relaxes and tones the body, helps to regain its natural balance. Your body will reawaken and come alive.

Therapeutic
Massage

A deep massage, which targets muscle tension. Helps to recover and regenerate the physiological functioning of the area affected by inflammation, wounds, sprains etc. Therapeutic massage can also rebalance the digestive and lymphatic systems, and brings benefits to the entire body.

Athletic
Massage

Aims to release specific tensions and prepare the body for physical activity. Helps in prevention and rehabilitation of muscle injuries.

Lymphatic
Drainage

Massage technique that stimulates lymphatic system and drains the toxins, while strengthening the immune system. Lymphatic drainage helps to accelerate scarring and reduces the risk of infections. It would also be useful to treat conditions such as varicose veins, cellulites and venous insufficiency (heavy legs).

Prenatal
Massage

Prenatal Massage offers relaxation tailored to your stage of pregnancy. Massage therapists will address the tension, aches, and pains caused by pregnancy to give expecting moms the gift of wellness they deserve—and need! This deep tissue massage calms the baby and dissipates stress, soothing sore muscles. It also improves lymphatic circulation to reduce swelling and discomfort. It is not recommended to start massage treatments prior 12 weeks of gestation, in order to diminish the risks for the fetus.
